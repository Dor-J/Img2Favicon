How it works

  1. Upload an image to the crop tool.
  2. Choose an aspect ratio or freeform mode and adjust the crop region.
  3. Download the cropped image in your chosen format.

Frequently asked questions

What aspect ratios are supported?

Free, 1:1, 16:9, 4:3, and 3:2 presets are available, plus freeform cropping.

Does cropping upload my photo?

No. Cropping is done locally in your browser.
