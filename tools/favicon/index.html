How it works

  1. Upload an image or switch to text mode and enter your letters.

Frequently asked questions

What files are in a favicon kit?

A complete kit includes favicon.ico, favicon.svg, PNG sizes (16, 32, 180, 192), site.webmanifest, and a ready-to-paste HTML snippet.

Does this upload my image?

No. Image and text favicons are generated entirely in your browser.

Can I create a favicon from text?

Yes. Enter letters, pick fonts and colors, and export the same multi-format kit as image-based favicons.

Is the favicon generator free?

Yes. Img2Favicon is free to use with no account required.
